# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

Be kind, be constructive, be respectful. Assume good intent from fellow contributors.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and addressed.
